Smart Context

Stop manually searching for files. Engram predicts what you need before you even ask, turning your editor into a proactive partner in your development productivity.

Context-Aware Autocomplete

Traditional autocomplete is limited to the current file or simple keyword matching. Smart Context analyzes your active cursor position, your recent file history, and your semantic index to retrieve entire blocks of relevant code from across your project. It's like having a co-pilot that knows your entire codebase by heart.

Relevance Ranking

Not all code is equal. When you're debugging a React component, you don't need SQL queries from your backend. Engram uses advanced re-ranking algorithms to filter and prioritize context, ensuring that the information presented to you (or your LLM) is hyper-relevant to your immediate task.

Flow State Preservation

Every time you switch tabs to search for documentation or an old function definition, you lose focus. By surfacing this information automatically in your sidebar or tooltip, Engram preserves your cognitive momentum, allowing you to code longer and cleaner.
