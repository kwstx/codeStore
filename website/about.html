About Engram

Engram is an independent, solo-developed project driven by a passion for privacy and productivity. My mission is to democratize code intelligence without compromising on data sovereignty.

The Mission

Modern AI tools require you to upload your intellectual property to the cloud. I believe there's a better way. By leveraging local hardware acceleration, we can bring the power of LLMs to your machine, ensuring your code never leaves your network.
