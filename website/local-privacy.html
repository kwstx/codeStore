Local Privacy

Security is not an afterthought; it's our foundation. Engram ensures your intellectual property never leaves your machine, providing enterprise-grade security for personal workflows.

Air-Gapped by Design

In an era of cloud reliance, Engram stands apart. We have architected our extension to function without any external API calls for its core logic. This means your proprietary code, sensitive keys, and intellectual property remain strictly within your local environment. It's as secure as your own hard drive.

Local LLM Support

We believe in the democratization of AI. Engram integrates seamlessly with local inference servers like Ollama and LM Studio. This allows you to leverage powerful models like Llama 3 or Mistral running directly on your GPU, ensuring that even your prompts and queries stay private.

Data Sovereignty

The semantic index we build—the mathematical map of your code—is stored locally in a highly optimized vector database. You have full control over this data. You can inspect it, delete it, or rebuild it at any time. We assert zero ownership over the insights generated from your work.
